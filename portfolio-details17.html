Project Story

This project involved developing comprehensive control and monitoring systems for a data center. The tasks included creating the architecture and electrical drawings for the control and monitoring systems, as well as designing the interface for a web-based SCADA (Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition) system for two sites within the data center. I developed a monitoring program to track power meters, temperature and humidity sensors, and two generators, as well as a controlling program for the data center's air conditioners. Additionally, I created a program to manage the operation of eight air conditioning units, ensuring they operated in a sequential manner to optimize cooling efficiency.

Hardware used:
  • 1 PLC
  • 18 Power meters
  • 4 Temp and Humd Sensors
  • 8 Air Conditioners
  • 2 Generator
  • Fire Alarm Systems
  • Access Door
  • CCTV
